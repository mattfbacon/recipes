# Pesto

Makes 250ml.

## Ingredients

- 80g basil, stemmed (measured without stems)
- 2 garlic cloves
- 50g parmesan, grated
- 150ml olive oil

## Instructions

1. Chop basil to a medium fineness.
1. Add garlic and chop together to almost the final fineness.
1. Add parmesan and chop to integrate.
1. Mix in olive oil.
